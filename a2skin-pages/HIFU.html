NEW DOUBLO 2.0 HIFU






DELIVERS UP TO 380% IMPROVED PERFORMANCE COMPARED TO TRADITIONAL HIFU ACROSS 14 INDICATIONS


Synergy effect

NEW DOUBLO 2.0 is the next generation of lifting technology. It introduces the world's first SD (Synergy Dot) technology, combining the benefits of Micro-Focused Ultrasound (MFU) and Radio Frequency (RF) simultaneously.

While traditional HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) primarily targets deeper layers for lifting, the addition of RF allows for simultaneous thermal energy delivery to the skin's surface and dermis. This dual-action approach maximizes collagen stimulation and skin tightening results in a single session with improved comfort and safety. New Doublo 2.0 targets multiple layers including the SMAS (Superficial Muscular Aponeurotic System) to provide a comprehensive non-surgical facelift effect.



Why choose New Doublo 2.0?

1. Synergy Effect: The combination of RF and MFU energies targets multiple skin layers simultaneously, providing both immediate tightening and long-term lifting.

2. Faster Treatment: The advanced FL (Focused Ultrasound Line) and SD (Synergy Dot) handpieces allow for more efficient treatment sessions compared to older devices.

3. Enhanced Safety: Built-in safety sensors (Contact Sensor and Motion Sensor) prevent overlapping shots and ensure consistent energy delivery, minimizing risks of side effects.

4. Versatility: With various cartridge depths, the New Doublo 2.0 can treat the forehead, eye area, cheeks, jawline, neck, and even body areas for comprehensive rejuvenation.

5. Reduced Discomfort: The synergy technology distributes heat more evenly, often resulting in a more comfortable experience for the patient than traditional single-modality HIFU.

6. No Downtime: This non-surgical procedure allows you to return to your daily routine immediately after treatment.

7. Long-lasting Rejuvenation: Continuous collagen regeneration leads to progressive improvements in skin elasticity and texture for months following the procedure.


Treatment Mechanism

By delivering precise thermal energy to the SMAS layer and the dermis, the New Doublo 2.0 triggers a natural wound-healing response. This leads to the contraction of existing collagen fibers and the synthesis of new ones, effectively "lifting" the face from within using state-of-the-art ultrasound and radio frequency synergy.
