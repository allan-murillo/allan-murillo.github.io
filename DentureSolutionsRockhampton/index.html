    Digital Denture Services

    Digital dentures are created using advanced 3D technology for greater accuracy, comfort, and consistency. This modern approach ensures a precise fit and a more natural-looking smile, with digital files stored for easy future adjustments or remakes.

    3D Mouth Scanning

    No more messy impressions. Our 3D mouth scanning captures highly accurate digital images of your gums and teeth, providing a faster, more comfortable, and more precise starting point for your denture treatment.

    3D Printed Dentures

    Using state-of-the-art 3D printing, we produce dentures with exceptional detail, strength, and fit. This technology allows for faster production and more consistent results compared to traditional methods.

    Milled Dentures

    Milled dentures are crafted from high-quality, solid discs using computer-guided milling machines. This process delivers superior durability, precision, and a smooth, natural finish that enhances both comfort and longevity.

    Fast Turnaround

    Our digital workflow allows us to deliver dentures faster than traditional methods—perfect for patients needing quick results without sacrificing quality.

    Precise Dentures

    With advanced scanning, digital design, and modern manufacturing technology, we ensure every denture is crafted with exceptional accuracy for optimal comfort, fit, and appearance.

    Digital Smile Design

    Digital Smile Design allows you to preview your new smile before treatment begins. Using advanced software, we create a precise visual simulation of how your dentures will look, helping you feel confident.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do I need a referral to visit Denture Solutions Rockhampton?

    No referral is needed. You can book an appointment directly with us anytime.

    How long does it take to make new dentures?

    Typically, it takes 1–2 weeks, depending on the type of denture and your individual needs. We also offer faster turnaround options when needed.

    Do you offer repairs and relines for broken or loose dentures?

    Yes, we provide same-day repairs and relines in most cases. Bring your denture in, and we will assess it immediately.

    Will my new dentures look natural?

    Absolutely. We design dentures to match your natural smile, facial shape, and preferred shade for the most comfortable and natural-looking fit.

    What should I do if my denture is causing discomfort?

    Book an adjustment with us. Do not try to fix it yourself—DIY fixes may cause more damage.

    Do you accept health fund claims?

    Yes, we accept major health funds, and HICAPS is available for instant claiming.
